About Appin Technology Lab...



Appin Technology, Coimbatore is a Training division of Ether Services, a company registered in Tamil Nadu. Ether Services started in the year 2013 with the goal of providing Software development Services in Web Design, Web Development, Mobile App Development and Quality Software Training.

The founder of this Company is Mohan Natarajan has quite an experience in Software Product Development, Mechanical Design (CAD), and Software Education. Gradually we entered allied software services areas such as Web Hosting and Staffing services. Ether Services played an important role in providing quality services and early-stage implementation of software development to Tier II and Tier III cities of Tamil Nadu.

Our Job oriented courses not only concentrate on Technology Training but also on assisting students in getting jobs.
Ether Services provides solutions to many start-ups.
